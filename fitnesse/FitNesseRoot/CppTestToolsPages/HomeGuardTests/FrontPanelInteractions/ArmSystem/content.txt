When Home Guard is not armed, pressing the arm button arms the system.  The user is prompted for a password for Arm and Disarm

!|Action Fixture|
|start|Front Panel Controller|
|check|display|READY|
|check| arm led | off|
|press| arm button|
|check|display|ENTER PIN|
|enter|digits|3141|
|press| enter button|
|check| arm led | on|
|check|display|ARMED|

Pressing it again disarms the system. 

!|Action Fixture|
|press| arm button|
|check|display|ENTER PIN|
|enter|digits|3141|
|press| enter button|
|check| arm led | off|
|check|display|READY|
