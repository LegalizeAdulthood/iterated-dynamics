 2.31 Gingerbreadman

  (type=gingerbreadman)

  This simple fractal is a charming example stolen from "Science of
  Fractal Images", p. 149.

               xnew = 1 - y + |x|
               ynew = x

  The initial x and y values are set by parameters, defaults x=-.1, y = 0.
